# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## What You Can Do with This App

FoodGram is a food sharing app that allows you to share the joy of food with users around the world. You can record and share delicious dishes and favorite food spots while building a global "Food Map" together.

Main features:
- Post and share photos of dishes
- Record restaurant locations on the map
- Browse and like other users' posts
- Follow users to customize your timeline
- Save posts to view later
- Post anonymously
- Multi-language support (Japanese, English, Korean, Chinese, Thai, Vietnamese, etc.)

## About Posting

The following information is required for posting:

- Food image(s)
- Food name
- Restaurant location
- Comment

### Food Images

- **You can post multiple images**. You can add multiple images per post.
- We look forward to seeing delicious-looking photos.
- We generally only allow food images.

### Food Name

- The food name does not need to be exact, but please use a name that other users can understand as much as possible.

### Restaurant Location

- Please add the location where you ate at the restaurant. Since we obtain data from Google Maps, the restaurant may not appear. In that case, please select "Other."
- If you ate at home, please select "Home Cooking."

### Comment

- Please write your honest thoughts about what you ate.
- Currently, we do not have any agreements with restaurants.

### Anonymous Posting

- You can choose to post anonymously when creating a post. If you post anonymously, your username will not be displayed.

- You can save posts you like.
- Tap the save button on the post detail screen to save it for later viewing.
- You can view saved posts from your profile screen, etc.

- You can "like" posts you enjoy.
- When you like a post, a notification will be sent to the post author.

## Follow Feature

- You can follow other users.
- Posts from users you follow will appear in your timeline.
- You can check the number of followers and following on the profile screen.

## How to Delete Your Account

- You can delete your account from the "Delete Account" option in the settings screen.
- **Once you delete your account, data cannot be recovered.**
- Please confirm that all your post data will be deleted before proceeding.
